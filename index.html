What is the acceptance rate?
The acceptance rate is the probability of your application being accepted. It is the ratio of the applicants admitted to the total number of people who submit their applications.
How to calculate acceptance rate?
To calculate acceptance rate you should use the following formula:
acceptance rate = (number of applicants accepted / total number of applicants) * 100
Why use acceptance rate?
The acceptance rate is used in order to know the probability of an application being accepted.
